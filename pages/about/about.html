About Me

Emecheta Shalom Sampson is a virtual assitant/email copywriter who hails from the eastern part of Nigeria. She's currently a web development student at Capacitybay, a U.S based information technology training and workforce development institution. She's the first daugher out of two girls in the family and is the second out of three children. She holds a B.sc degree in Human Anatomy and graduated from Alex Ekwueme Federal University, Ndufu-Alike, Ebonyi State in the year 2021. She served at General Hospital in Calabar, Cross River State during her National youth service in the year, 2022. She lives in Uyo, Akwa Ibom and is into fashion designing. She also started a baking business in the year 2022 and it has been going smoothly. When she isn't working or studying, she spends her time binge-watching horror movies, writing or travelling. She also enjoys reading innovative materials at her leisure.
